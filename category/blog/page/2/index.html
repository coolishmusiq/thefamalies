The power of having hope in your life

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 10, 2020
  • No Comments on The power of having hope in your life

If you feel like your life is not moving. You have been at the same level for many years. It is time you step up your game. You can have everything you want just by believing you can.

Stop trying to belong, be selfish for once. Just focus on yourself.

Have planned moments with yourself and understand it is time for you to change. Even if it means cutting all your friends, social media, and everything that has been distracting you from starting your journey.

Friends can be a terrible inspiration. Think about what is it about your friend you love. What is it they bring to you or they are taking? It can be hard sometimes separating from the things we love. It needed some time so we can focus on us and accomplishing our goals.  

The power of having hope

I believe reading this today can take you to another level. Not only in life but also have respect in your relationship. 

You can take the information that I give you today. Mine it and make it yours. Reading alone won’t be enough. Start doing and start moving forward.

The wrong way of looking into success is to look at it as a destination. The finish line that one day you want to get to. No, don’t look at it like that look at it as the life that you choose.

Look at it as something that yours already. If you want to be rich, start working hard like a rich man. Act as if you are there already.

Today I want you to believe that I can and I will be an overcomer. This will be true, you will get all you want and more.

We are all created to live in hope. That God has plans about me which will become a reality. So, I can become better for me and my family.  

We were meant to live in hope, joy, and peace. Focus on hope today will be like rehearsing for your future freedom.

If we have hope, a lot of things happen. Your life may be in a tough time. If you have hope you don’t get offended, you don’t feel rejected or hurt.

All those offenses can’t penetrate you. When there is an atmosphere of hope around you.  When you meet people full of hope, you think life is kinder to them. But it only because they process things differently through courage.

Use your imagination the right way and it will lead you to the right things. Take the seed of faith. There is so much potential.

There will still be unknown. If you don’t quite and grow worried. Everything around you perform better when there is hope. Your partner, kids everything. 

Never forget the relationship works best when there is hope. Choose life

  • Hope is a risky business and will get you disappointed most of the time. If you are going to live hopelessly, you will never get disappointed. But with courage even if you get disappointed, you are still going to simplify your purpose on earth. Whether your dreams come true. You trust yourself and your future. You will problem solve better when you have courage inside you. It might be a risky business, but great for problem-solving.
  • Hope is your choice. There are no hopeless circumstances, there are just hopeless people. What we need to do is control. Know what your thought are tonight. You get to choose what you focus your thought on. Whatever you focus your mind on. Will have a spike in your life the next day and the rest of your life.  
  • Hope is not an illusion. It’s not just wishful thinking but linked to the promises of God.
  • The power of words, can kill and give life. If you start a relationship. You only need your words, how powerful life can be. Even changing your life can only take a few words. You need to be very careful with the words you speak. What you want more, talk about it more. If you want more beauty in your life, take about it more. If you are in a situation of hopelessness. Talking about the positive and having hope can change your life. See your life with hope. Do not allow circumstances to dictate your life.
  • Hope is soil. It really saying I trust in him. Farms care for two things, seed, and the soil. If you take faith and hope, use it together and operate in love. You will be unstoppable. Do not wait for your certain circumstances to get better. Have hope now 

Stop making excuses to yourself

Now after building hope. It is time for you to stop making excuses.  Stop saying I want this, I want that. Now it is time for you to say I will get this no matter what.

Not I wish, but I will. No matter how far away you are from it. Believing you will get it, will change your like. You may not get what you want. You will get other things in a process of getting it what you wanted.

Stop allowing yourself to get off the hook and have a commitment to the thing you want. Stop being a victim and blaming the past or your current condition. Hard work is all that you need in this world to get what you want. You don’t even need talent.

Stop blaming things that are out of your control. Start using the things that are in your control. Ask your self what you need to do to be more like a champion. Instead of being a victim all the time. There are people who have originated in a worse situation than you and me. But today we praise them for what they have archived with nothing.  

Start doing, you will figure out things as you go. The way you do one thing is the way you do every. If you make excuses for yourself, you also make excuses in your relationship, you also make an excuse in your family, etc. when everything starts to go south in your life. You blade a lot of things. It is you who is making your life worse, by putting wrong energy in everything.

The way to get a lot of things done in your life is by you saying I will. Even if it gets hard. I’m going to continue, anyway. If you stop giving excuses to yourself. You will see a change. 

How to stop avoiding the thing you want

Stop avoiding things you want to do. Stop being afraid to do things you want in your life. You keep coming up reasons not to do them, that is avoidance.  Fight avoidance and fight that fire inside you. Do you have dreams? Yet you avoid the first step you must take. Which will lead you into your dreams? 

You know people who can help. You avoid them, stop doing that today. You know everything you have been avoiding. Today you must sit down and ask yourself. Am I a bigger person than that or a failure who will never win?

Will you allow that small step to hold you back? Or are you going to get back in that momentum that you deserve?

Stop avoiding and ask yourself how can I attack this. Stop being lazy and defensive. Things you want, you must attack them all.

Ask yourself every day. What is it for me today? Not 3 days from now, not 4 months from now, and not when you hope it turns out.

Now! What must happen this day! Prioritize you have people who are going to depend on you. Be focus!

Always focus on what matters in your life. If your phone distracts you turn off. You can’t afford any distractions.

Have a plan, if you don’t plan you will burnout in life.

Everything you need to know about apologizing

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 9, 2020
  • No Comments on Everything you need to know about apologizing

How to apologize to someone you offended and the power of apology. Saying I am sorry for hurting you can mean a lot to some people. But what I see is that it is very hard to apologize to a woman. Because even when you say forgive me, my love. They can hardly take that as a good apology and tell you to don’t say it if you don’t mean it. You think I have apologized, what more does she wants.

Sometimes we don’t even know how to apologize the proper way as men. Especially if dating, your partner will even tell you, to tell her, that you are sorry. But an apology is not something that you ask for, the person who did you wrong must always apologize. But not on your term because he must mean what he is saying. Not because you tell him to.

There are things you should never apologize for and there are things you should. It is your choice to beg for forgiveness. Women tell us when we upset them and we believe them. But do we apologize all the time, even when it was just a misunderstanding? Let talk about that.

The do’s and the don’ts of apologizing

The first thing we must understand when dating. You will never be loved 100% the way you want. I don’t care if you have the richest man in the world or just a normal guy. Take out the thought people must be 100%. Because even you will be 100% in them.

What are the do’s and don’ts of apology it a broad question. I am not an expert in apologizing and believe no one is an authority in apologizing. With apologizing will never be perfect. We all struggle in this, some do it more than they should. Some don’t even know when to say it. Some don’t even know how to react if you apologize to them.

The problem with most of us is when something happens. It is hard to accept that on me! Even though we are taught to say I’m sorry. Most of us don’t know how to say that well enough. So it means something to a person we are saying sorry to.

The apology is a gift and it tool that can be used to restore a relationship. More often to people we are close to. If you apologize to someone you say I care about you and I put the relationship first.

I care more about fixing your feeling than my pride.  Apologizing is so difficult and it so humbling. Sometimes in saying I’m sorry, you don’t have the perfect words. You also fear saying wrong things which can make the situation worse.

It’s hard to control such situations and sometimes. I just wish I can hide and don’t apologize. Just forget anything ever happened and that not possible too.

But in this situation what helps is to think about the relationship. How much it means to you. How much you love that person and how much are you willing to keep it.

If you really care about them and you believe them. When they say you upset me. You have no choice, but to apologize. Even if you know you are not wrong. It not about you being right. But you saving your relationship. Also, you caring for that person.

You must admit you are not perfect. Even though that hard to admit to other people.

Thing we can do to apologize well

  • The first thing you must do when apologizing is to use I. if you are saying you are sorry you need to take the blame. You can’t say you are sorry. But still blaming the person you want to forgiveness from. Women sometimes apologize to expose your bad behavior. Which is not a proper way to apologize to someone. Your apology must be about you, not the other person. Before apologizing it helps to sit down with yourself first. Understand why you need to apologize and how will you do it.
  • Find the right time to do it. You need to make sure the other person is in a state to listen. The perfect time to say I’m sorry is immediately when it happens. But I understand sometimes it can take some time for us to realize no, this was wrong. The minute you realize, say you are sorry. Because the sooner the better. Because sometimes you can feel that you are not ready to apologize. Claim your self down and when you feel you can do it. Do it!
  • See it on the side of the other person. Because most of the time what makes it hard to apologize. We focus too much on what we think and forget about the other person. You may have a unique perspective. However, just because you have a different perspective. Really doesn’t mean you are right based on their perspective.
  • Sorry without a change is not enough. If you say I’m sorry to someone. Your action must change afterward.  If you are sorry, it means you will change your behavior. Otherwise, there is no need for you to apologize, if you won’t change. Above I said the aim to apologize is to repair and if you don’t change. You are still harming the relationship. Change is so important. Because if you did something. That oven yourself agrees it is wrong. Yet you are doing it again. It really brings attention to you as a person.

Things we don’t do when apologizing

  • The first thing that I see all the time. When someone is trying to apologize they use but. Don’t say But! Because when you say I’m sorry but! Your but is the only thing that person receives. If you apologize to me. I like that and for a moment I think you care about my feeling. However, the minute you say but I forget everything you said.
  • Saying you are sorry. While you are busy doing the other things.  This really doesn’t count. If you apologize, you must pay attention to your words and be in that moment of apologizing.
  • Don’t expect someone to forgive you. Just after saying you are sorry. Even if you know you said I’m sorry respectfully.  But the pleasant thing is that you did. It will also give you relief if they don’t forgive you. They have a right to do so and that on them. Understand forgiveness is a process, and it happens over time.  Some people need to see you change before they can accept your apology. Some accept it right away. I am one of those. If you say sorry to me, I forgive you immediately. If I don’t forgive you now, I might never. But the rest is on me. What more important is that you came to me and humbled yourself.
  • Avoid apologizing using text. Try the phone call or in person. What I noticed about the text is that some people can read it wrong. You can say something and mean it. But the other person can read it and get the wrong viewpoint. Beside it more respectful to say in person or in a call. Rather than just text.
  • Don’t have expectations of the other person. There are times in life where. Someone will deeply hurt you, and you sit there waiting for an apology that will never come. Sometimes you might get it. But it won’t be the one you hoped for. So, it really helps to don’t have high hopes with things like someone saying sorry to you. You can teach yourself to forgive people without them saying I’m sorry. Because some don’t say I’m sorry in words. But they do something nice for you to say sorry. You on the other hand sit there waiting for an apology. But they think they have apologized. You can not change some people the only person you can change is yourself.
  • Never apologize if you feel, you don’t want too, and you are sure. You were not wrong. Instead, tell your partner. You understand what you did was wrong and you will do better next time.

The power on apologizing

Harvard Medical College made a study about the apology and the power behind forgiveness. People who are hurt or humiliated often wait for forgiveness. Why do we even need an apology?

  • The first thing to be sure the same thing will never happens
  • To understand if something was done intentionally or not.
  • To repair our self
  • To earn respect
  • And to put that thing in the past

This video explains how hard apologizing in men can be. But yet it is the most important thing for women. If you don’t say I’m sorry. You build bridges with the person you are in a relationship with. You apology it the only thing that can fix things.

We sometimes think apologizing will make us look weak. But most of the time once you do it, it does the opposite effect. Most of the time in the relationship is wasted talking about the past. But this sometimes can be avoided. If we teach our self to say I’m sorry when we are wrong. We can move on with our lives.

Remember to say I’m sorry and remember to say thank you. This will change your life.

Over apologizing

We understand by now how important it is to say I’m sorry. But do we really need to apologies all the time? If we do what this says about us.

Once you teach yourself this, you can overdo it sometime. The problem with apologizing for everything. It creates this picture of you doing everything wrong. Just because you know you are going to say I’m sorry. The more you say it, the more it feels unreal. 

Over apologizing can undermine you. It can also hurt your self-esteem, and no one is doing that to you. But you do that to yourself.

So, you need to find the balance. Don’t overdo it, overdoing it doesn’t always mean you say it over and over.

If you did something, and your partner is mad. All she need is your word that sincerely come from your heart. The roses can be nice. But once you start washing her car, calling her 300times, telling everyone to talk to her, showing up at her work, etc.

You are overdoing it and that not what she wants. That might frustrate her even more.

When the apology is not accepted

When we say I’m sorry, it doesn’t always go as planned. Sometimes it can backfire hard and not always right away. It can backfire when you said you are sorry and then you went through and did the same thing all over again.

Sometimes your partner will be mad at you. But with no sufficient proof. Then you ask for forgiveness and it proves to her you did it. Which gets her even crazier.

They can use your sorry against you.

We don’t like to request for forgiveness and find that they do not accept our forgiveness. But that does and will happen. There are things you can do if your admission of guilt is not accepted. Thich can help or stop the situation from getting worse.

  1. You must never take your apology back. Which you can take by your reactions. Because if you say I’m sorry, you do it for the right reasons. If they reject you that on them.
  2. Don’t be defensive or try to remind them how many times time they have been wrong, and you have forgiven them. It not about them this time, but you.
  3. Give them space so they can process it.
  4. Show your apology with your actions
  5. Re-try visit some other time. Remind them you meant what you said and you are still sorry.

Exchange of apologies

Sometimes your partner is wrong 50% and you also wrong in a way. But you must apologize to fix your partner and your partner need to apologize to you.

An outstanding example of this is when you did something wrong. Then your partner says hateful things to you just because you are wrong. After you apologize and your partner forgives you. The questions come flying in your head. If your partner really meant what she said to you.

Now even though the problem was about you. But they also need to apologize to you. So, you can heal too. Because what they said to you is very hurtful and you can’t stop thinking about it. 

What usually helps me so I don’t say hurtful things in the other person. I look into myself first and have respect for myself. Me respecting myself means I can say no, you can’t say such things to others. Even if want to say it and it in my head. But I don’t say it. Because I respect them more importantly, I respect me!

Exchanging apologies is great. Because in the end it not about you. If you made that person feel humiliated you own him too. Every time someone says I’m sorry. It doesn’t end there, you need to think about how you reacted to them.

Don’t get defensive about it or make a justification. The reason you did wrong is that of what they did. NO, You had many ways to handle the situation, but you have chosen to be hurtful.

It is common for people who always expect people to apologize, to don’t say I’m sorry too. Or if they do say there are sorry, they include but you have. Or say I am only apologizing because…..

Thich is total wrong and don’t qualify as a proper apology.

The other thing which you must know about exchanging apologies. which I might have talked about early. If you say I’m sorry that doesn’t mean it fixes everything right away. So, they might not apologize back right away. It can take a few hours sometimes even longer than that.

Making a big deal out of the small stuff

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 7, 2020
  • No Comments on Making a big deal out of the small stuff

I and my wife were talking about the other last night. It seems like people like to make a big deal out of the small stuff.

She used to be like this, and there was a time where I was like this. But as you grow older, you realize this not how you live with others. We all make mistakes, there is no need to fight about everything.

Because having a tendency to make a big deal out of minor matters can ruin a relationship. I always tell people not to start a fight unless it beyond your powers.

If you make big deal out of everything. That when your partner might say you talk too much. This can be the beginning of an unhealthy relationship.

So, today I’m saying, Stop Making A Big Deal Over the Little Things.

Find good habits for your relationship. This is the nasty habit and you must find a way to of breaking this habit. It does more harm than good.

There are many good patterns you can teach yourself. Same as bad ones, if you want a healthy relationship. You must stop the bad patterns.

If you make a big deal out of every minor situation. This means you controlling, and no one enjoys being with a controlling partner.

Once you stop making a big deal out of everything. This is when you will learn the skill to solve problems, without fighting with your partner.

Sometimes you need to know your partner is old enough to know right for wrong.  No need to remind him no, this is wrong every time.

He already knows what he did was wrong. 

Always ask yourself is this worth my time. It is easy to shout at people until we lose them.

I understand it can happen, that your partner thinks it not a big deal. But it is for you. That also happens all the time.

In a case, where your partner thinks it not a big deal and you think otherwise. Talk to them like the adult. They would understand no need to fight every time.

If you have talked to your partner several times and he keeps doing the same. Then we can make it a big deal.!

Preventing fighting from ruining your relationship

The first thing which is wrong. We think relationship and marriages are hard.

So, when our relationship has problems. We take this as normal and don’t see it as a problem. So, we can deal with it.

We get advice. Relationships are hard work. If you want to stay in a relationship, fight to keep it.

But you don’t fight for a relationship, which is health. If you must always fight for your relationship, that not health. Some day you might realize no, I can’t take it anymore.

The problem started when you believed fight is normal in a relationship. A single fight can be normal. But if it happens often, that not normal.

You will have arguments, issues, and hard times. But should not escalate to fights. Because in a good relationship there is clear communication and problem-solving strategy.

Consistent fighting and making a big deal out of small things. That not normal and it could be your strategy of dealing with fight is not working. It can also mean one of you is falling out of love.

  • Falling out of love can make you fight for things that don’t matter.
  • It can also mean you are angry about something. That you haven’t addressed yet. For example, they do not please you in bed. So everything upsets you.
  • You could fight about something that happened in the past. You have talked about it and it passed. But months later you re-think it and be angry. This time you show your anger in other things. Even though it is still connected to what happened in the past.

This can make your relationship unhealthy, and you might not even realize it. If this continues it creates a bigger problem. Can even end the relationship.

Break the circle of fighting

Deal with what in front of you right now.

What I mean is, don’t talk about what happened yesterday or the other month. Instead, fix only what you are talking about now.

If you had a problem and talked about it. Then your partner apologized. If he apologized and you saw, he meant what he said. Never bring that up again. Even when you are fighting about something else.

If it is something that happened last week. It must be completely off the table. You fix everything as it happens and moving on with your relationship. Without allowing problems to add up on top on one another.

Keeping a record of everything is wrong. First, you did this second, you did that, now you are doing this. That all wrong!

Because you have talked about it and came up with a solution. Why are you even mad about it for a week or a month later?

When you keep bringing up things. Which happened in the past, over and over. You are not allowing your relationship to move forward.

When you focus at the moment. Your communication skills will improve.

If you keep everything that happened in the past. You will win the fight. But your partner knows what you did in the past, too. So, if he is also bringing everything too, he will win. This means the conversation is not going forward and the relationship.

If you forgive someone it means you put that in the past. If it too much to forgive don’t continue with that relationship.

Words you need to avoid when arguing in the relationship.

Let me assume the reason for you to read this article is that you are in a similar situation. Where you and your partner are always fighting and one is making a big deal out of everything.

I want to warn you about two words you should avoid when fighting. These words are 1 always 2 never!

It is something you never thought about before. But it is very damaging.

It can take some time to break the habit. Knowing this is something I should stop will make you one step ahead.

Let me make an example of what I mean.

  • You are always starting a fight
  • You always messing things up
  • We always do what you want us to do
  • You always make fun of me
  • I always must do everything
  • You always ignore me
  • You never listen to me
  • You never cared
  • You never help
  • You are never romantic
  • You never put effort into anything
  • You never let me choose

Think about it. The chances of never and always being accurate are small to none. We use always and never when we frustrated or angry about something.

What these words do. They also make your partner frustrated too. Because even though your partner was wrong. Your partner won’t agree with what you are saying. This can start a separate fight on top of the one. You are already fighting about.

Two, it puts extra blame on your partner. For example, if you say your partner always mess thing up. This means since the day you meant them. They always mess thing up and that such a wrong thing to say.It not fair to say because it isn’t true.

Get to the root of the problem. Solve that problem without involving everything that happened before.

If you don’t get to the root of the problem. You leave that problem unsolved. Because instead of solving it. You brought back the old one. Even those were not solved. Because you also brought the older ones.

This becomes a chain of unsolved problems, and they will keep coming up. Causing you to fight all the time!

This can make you make a big deal out of small things. Because a lot of problems are stuck in your head unsolved.
Let us say your partner is talking about something you do that they don’t like. But they are not fighting with you yet. Telling you what they don’t like.

If you respond by saying, you always picking a fight. This means you don’t care about their feelings and you are reversing the problem to them.

The next thing that is going to happen. The fight will worsen. The other partner may even become more frustrated. Because you show you don’t even care.

So, this way of handling things solves no problem. It makes things worse!

If your partner use always or never in you. What then?

There will be a time where your partner says you always do this, or you never do this. You as a person who already knows. How damaging those words can be. You must have a different manner.

If your partner says. You always pick a fight. You must keep calm and say come sit down here, let talk about it.

I’m sorry you feel that way. That I always pick a fight. I know we need to be better in handling our problems. Show your partner you care about their feelings.

Ask them what is it moving forward that they want you to do. To solve this problem.

You are solving a problem. Instead of reversing the problem to the other partner or bringing up the past.

Understand what exactly is bordering your partner right now. What role can I play moving forward to make things better for us? How can we make this better together?

If you talk to your partner like this. They feel

  • You are hearing
  • You respect their feelings
  • You care
  • You are working together as a team.

By doing this, it can cool the other partner way faster. Showing you care, makes the other person feel loved and acknowledged. This is why you are together.

Your partner doesn’t want to be angry. They are not speaking to you to annoy you. They are trying to solve the problem. So don’t make the problem worse.

If you tell your partner “you never listen to me”. This means they have never listened to you, ever since you started dating. How true is that?

It like you are telling your partner, they are always wrong and the only reason you are still together is you. Which is not fair!

Each person has a unique mind and voice. Using always and never will make your relationship to grow apart. This will cause a lot of unnecessary fights. Without you understanding where they have come from. 

Tips for fighting fair

This article is about a relationship. Where partners fight all the time. The relationship where everything turns in to a big deal. The aim is to help you reduce fight in your relationship.  Because fight can turn even a perfect relationship into a sour one.

So, we need to look at this problem in many viewpoints and give you unique solutions.

There are many ways partners deal with the conflict. Both partners must not try to win the conversation. Without interest in solving the problem.

Both partners want the fight to end. But it cannot if the fight is going in the wrong direction.  

If there is a problem in your relationship. But both of you are defensive. It is less likely you will move forward.  

How do you fight fair?

  1. There is a saying don’t go to bed angry. It’s time we rethink that. We must teach our self to take a break if something is frustrating us. Even if that means going to bed angry, it is fine we will talk about it tomorrow. But this will help us avoid saying things we don’t mean because we are angry. It also gives us time to think. The next day you might even realize. I don’t want to talk about this anymore, it is not worth the time. This will help us stop saying things in a mean way, harsh and hurtful. Saying things that can’t be forgetting.
  2. The second method of fighting fair is the one we talk about early to stop using the words always and never. Because they are not accurate, and they do more harm than good.
  3. Find a solution. Stop fighting back your partner. Work together so you can find a solution.
  4. Focus on the present. Forget about the past wrongs.
  5. Never interrupt your partner. Let your partner finish and give your viewpoint.
  6. Apologize if needed.
  7. Never fight in front of your kids. Children a copycat, if you fight opposite to them. They might fight the other kids at school. You might blame them. But it is you who are teaching them the wrong things.
  8. Watch your words.

Conclusion

If your relationship is always boiling and you fight all the time. Learn to listen to your partner. Forget who is wrong for a moment and focus on how we can solve this.

Be loving to each other. Find the strategy to solve problems in your relationship. But you can’t come up with a strategy during fights.

Coming with a strategy will need both partners to be cool and claim. Talk about your bad habits that are costing a relationship. When you are both in the right space.

Communication is the key, talk to your partner, and a find solution.

Make sure, you ready are you. Find out is he dating someone currently. Check if has he made enough effort and do you love him or what you love what he did. If you were to date him will you complete him and what is his HIV status?

Starting a relationship is a huge decision. But sometimes we just jump into a relationship without asking our self what am I getting myself into. Sometimes a relationship can work just fine. Though sometimes that can be a death sentence.

Just because a man came to you and told you what he wants. Doesn’t mean he meant every word. The average men stop at least 3 girls per day and say the same words to all of them.

Now how can you easily trust a guy? I have found myself in a position where I talk to a girl, thinking I like her.

Then 3 weeks later, I don’t want to be part of that relationship. Why? Sometimes we as guy enter a relationship just because someone looks good.

After we sleep with her that when we realize we never wanted a relationship.

As a female, you need to be careful before you start a relationship.

Check yourself, check the person you are about to date!

As you grow, you should worrier about wasting time. If you enter a relationship and a year later you realize it not for you.

Now let us say you don’t have enough proof to tell if they cheated or not. How they react will tell if they are guilty or not.

The most common sign is that they try to justify everything. They usually cannot answer your question and involve a lot of things under one question.

If you didn’t do it, why would you even care that much about being in details? Not only they justify everything. But they get emotional too. They think you want to fight them, even if you were asking in a good-mannered.

After that, it becomes hard for them to sleep because they are guilty and still trying to come up with solutions. It like something is eating them.

They also avoid spending time with you and the eye contact. They might even ignore your call cause they fear you will ask them questions.

That how you know they are guilty and hiding something.

Cheating ruins a relationship

Cheating is the worst thing you can do to someone you love. It’s something you should never do, and it is very painful.

After you cheat on someone it changes the way they look at you. They don’t trust you anymore, and they now see you as a lie. Can they recover from that? Yes, but it will take time, a very long time.

If you cheat, not only you disappoint your partner. But you disappoint everyone. Your family, your partner family, kids,…everyone. Because once you grow, we expect you to be responsible and do the right things.

Worse, it takes years to rebuild. So why cheat? Let not forget you stand a chance to lose a relationship. That would mean you wasted all those years building a relationship for nothing.

Even if you don’t lose a relationship, you can still get STI. Now you and your partner are sick just because you couldn’t be responsible.

Cheating ruin a relationship and it goes deeper than you think.

Conclusion

I wish you can someone who loves you and respects you the way you want. Who never even tries to cheat on you. Because you deserve better than that. Being in love is a blessing, and to be loved by someone who never wants to lose you is what we all fight for.

Can distance kill a relationship? How to prevent it?

  • Post date August 19, 2020
  • No Comments on Can distance kill a relationship? How to prevent it?

Yes, distance can kill a relationship. Though you can talk every day over the phone. Lack of physical interaction can be a problem. Communicating can help, but does not solve all problems.

If you are in love with a person who validates physical interaction. The more time you spend apart, the more they will lose value. Walking hand in hand with your partner means a lot for some people, chatting all day doesn’t do much for them.

If you miss your partner and say “I wish I was there” that can be a pleasant thing to say but not for long. If you say that over and over. Then it gets annoying!

Movies make long-distance looks so simple, but in actual life it complicated. Words need to be followed by actions. Words alone won’t get far. Spending time with your partner is priceless.

Traveling cost

Traveling costs also play a huge role in killing long-distance relationships. If you must pay high fees to visit someone every month. Over time, you realize you can cover a lot of things with that money.

My advice if you can pay high traveling fees just to see that one person. The best you can do is just move with them.

You will save money and all that long-distance drama.

Living without sex

While one can stay without sex, the longer you stay without it. The more you will think your partner is doing it with someone else. The common issue with girls they like to accuse their boyfriends of cheating with 0 evidence.

Living without sex will also increase the chances of unnecessary fights. You can live without sex, but it won’t be a happy life.

Sex is many things in a relationship. If you both cannot do sex because of the distance. This means you might lose the chance of having children in time.

Life changes

Life has changed a lot over the years. Technology has changed the long-distance game. Even though you can use Skype or Google Hangouts, for video chat it’s still nothing compared to a dinner and movie date.

Tech has given long-distance partners the chance to talk 24.7, while this sounds like a grand thing. But talking every day and meet once in a while actually might have a negative effect on relationships.

In a short-term technology can save a long-distance relationship, but in the long term, it does more harm than good.

It hard to maintain the long distance with or without technology.

Signs your long distance is dying

Although we don’t like long-distance. We can’t run away from the fact someone is still involved. How can you see if your relationship is at risk?

This is important in long-distance because you might wait for someone who has moved on. If you see this sign, it can help to end a relationship before things get ugly.

  • Every conversation is turning in to an argument.
  • Reducing communication time over the phone.
  • Delaying the dates for the visits.
  • When you meet your partner, it feels like you are with a stranger.
  • Losing hope in a relationship.
  • No one is willing to compromise.

Third option

We know the most long-distance relationships won’t work. For this reason, most people keep options open while in a relationship since most relationships often fail.

If your partner thinks he/she is attractive, they think about the third option. Then just long-distance relationship. The distance can work for a few months, but as your partner has the second or third option. The risk in your relationship grown big time.

Especially when time gets hard, people give up easily in long-distance. Then in just a normal relationship. Having a negative thought about the relationship is enough to kill the relationship.

But in long-distance confusion and fear of wasting your time can be the feelings that you can’t run from.

How do I keep a distance from ruining my relationship?

Long-distance is very complicated and will need you both to be loyal, trust, and believe in each other. Most long-distance fail in a year or fast than that, however, some can make it work even though it difficult.

These are the thing that can help your relationship during long-distance

  • Get married people to respect the ring more, even when you are away.
  • Send pictures, make it a habit to send pictures every time you take new ones. If you both have kids, also send pictures for kids too.
  • Talk constantly, but not always. The mistake people do is to think just because there are on long-distance now, they must talk every day. Don’t only talk when it makes sense.
  • If you are both in a long distance because of work. One of you must try to get a work transfer as soon as possible.
  • Don’t say things you can’t say face to face over the phone. Respect your partner just because they are now far doesn’t mean you must say bad things over the phone you can’t tell in person.
  • Send gifts and followers. Just because you far do not mean you can’t send gifts home. Technology is very helpful if used the right way. You can even buy lunch using MR D or UberEATS for your partner while you are not around.
  • See it as an opportunity. While you are in a distance, focus on self-development. You can start learning, get a driver’s license, etc. Don’t just sit and wait for your partner, develop yourself.
  • Have rules. Most people skip this part, but it is necessary. For example, can one date while in the distance or can they use things like sex toys? You need to have rules and commit to them.
  • Avoid dirty talk. Most people think because you are far now you must send nudes and talk dirty constantly. But doing that will make you wish for sex if you can’t get it. That when frustrations kick in.
  • Don’t be selfish and careless. What do I mean by that? I mean going out with friends and late-night drinks. While your partner is not doing any of that and patiently waiting for you.
  • Have a goal you both want to achieve.
  • You must know when the distance will end. No relationship can stay on long-distance forever. So, it must be clear when it is going to end.
  • Know each other’s schedules. This can be helpful because you will know when to call and not to.
  • Spend more time with your family. While your partner is away trying to be available to your family and his.
  • Discuss how you deal with pressure. You both in a long-distance pressure will be there, discuss how you deal with it.
  • Talk about big issues in person, avoid them over the phone.
  • Visit often even when you don’t have money. The other mistake people do is to think they only need to go home when they got paid. If you have money for transport, go home your company will mean more.
  • Don’t ignore your instincts if you think something is wrong, pay attention.

How do you express love to your family?

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date August 7, 2020
  • No Comments on How do you express love to your family?

How do you express your love to your family members? Do you give them a gift? Do you give them a back scratch? Do you tell them you really appreciated something they did? Do you schedule a special time in your day?

Love your parents

Before you love anyone else in your family start with your parents. Respect them and give them love. No one in your family will love you if you are mean to your parents.

Develop a positive attitude toward your parents. They are the reason you even have a family. You may disagree with what your parents tell you from time to time.

Please! If you disagree with them, don’t be rude about it.

Spend time with them, even if you have your own place. Visit your parents and spend time with them.

Family comes first

Most blessings we get depend on the relationship we have with our family’s. If you are someone that believes in God like me. Before you ask anything on God do good for your family first. You must always be reliable, don’t make promises you can’t keep.

The pleasant thing about the family, you don’t have to pretend to be someone that you are not. They know you and they love you the way you are.

Positive mental attitude

A lot of things will happen in our families. Always try to keep a positive mental attitude whenever you can. Embrace life even when you have obstacles.

Life becomes unfair and hard for all of us. But if you keep a positive attitude, your family will appreciate you even more.

Why you shouldn’t have a negative mental attitude in your family? If you have a negative attitude, you think worse of everything.

Even if your family loves you, your mind can make you believe they don’t. It also makes smaller problem to become bigger problems.

Take your kids out

Kids love going out but they can’t because we as parents are the only ones with that power to take them out.

It always nice to take your kids out to different places so they can explore. You can all go out as a family with your husband and kids.

Do lunch, maybe go to a mini-town because kids love mini-towns.

Mini-town

But mini-town are not the only places kids enjoy. You can do your own research based on your location.

Be there when your presence is required

Sometimes your presence is enough. Families don’t always require you to bring something. Just be there when they need you that enough.

Some family members choose to stay away, maybe because of unpleasant experiences which happened in the past.

Be loyal

Loyalty is everything home, work, and in a relationship.  So, for your family to take you more seriously, you must stand for your words and avoid breaking promises.

You must be loyal to your family, which can also mean to be faithful. Try to provide them with what makes them happy. If you are lucky to have a job, you must put it in mind that you work for your family.

You must stand for your brother and sister and the entire family if they are in trouble. Give your family emotional support and improve your loyalty.

Loyalty is something which we teach ourselves, and over time we get better at it. Don’t forget your family also needs spiritual support.

Understand family moods

They will test your loyalty several times in your family. Family moods are very common and you need a plan to deal with them.

Some family member doesn’t like to be told what to do and not to do. That way you might request them to do something for you and that when the moods kick in.

But they can be moody for several reasons, I’m just making an example. Since moods are changed by a lot of things.

It wise to give your family members space if you see they are not in a good mood. Talk to them later, once they cool off. There is nothing wrong with bad moods. No one feels cheerful all the time. Just try not to make things worse.

Know what your family love

Different families love uncommon things, some love music, fishing, braai day, some movies, etc. To express love to your family you must know what they love as the entire family and individuals.

You will also need to know the birth date for each member. This can help you with arranging a surprise party and buying gifts in time.

Holiday times like Christmas are hard if you don’t know what your family likes.

Help one another

While every family can be different, most families depend on one another and help one another.

Families come in different sizes, your family might be big or small. However, if you want to express love to your family.

Teach them to help one another and be a leading example. What really makes a family is the love, care, and concern that members show for one another.

Be surprising

Don’t allow yourself to do the same thing over and over. Learn new things. The family loves surprises.  If you do pleasant things for them don’t expect them to do the same. Just do things out of your go hearth.

Yes, some will return the favor but don’t expect it from them.

Buy Gifts

Gifts are the obvious and popular way to express love to your family.  But not all of buying them. You need to learn the language of gifts. The fact we are not born with a giving nature or you were not given enough present growing up. Does not mean you must not buy a present for your family.

But don’t give a gift for the wrong reasons. Wrong reasons can be to compete with other givers in your family or to earn a reputation as a giver.

Many people give in order to feed their egos. Which is not cool you must always buy a gift for the right reason. The major reason is to express love.

Conclusion

Just express love to your family any way you can. Family is a gift on its own, appreciate that while you can. Your family needs your commitment and love. Show up if need and be there for each other, never give up on them.

Family is not only important but also everything. No family is perfect, every family spends time apart but makes time if needed. Forget about everything else, just go home, and love your family.
