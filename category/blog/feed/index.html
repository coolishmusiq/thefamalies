Fighting phone addiction

Social media apps are created so we can use them all the time.

They collect data from everyone and use that data in us. So, we can be looked at by our phones every day. They can sell their product fast. It is your job to protect yourself from this addiction.

If you are on social media and you see sponsored posts. Someone is benefiting from that. If you are on YouTube and you see ads. Someone is making money through those ads.

So, it obvious they all want us to be addicted so, they can make more money. If I can give you one tip that will change your life forever.

It is simple, turn of all your notification. Just turn off all off them. Because they are the ones that trick you to check your phone all the time. If you use Facebook all the time.

Go to your settings – go to apps – go to Facebook – go to notification

  • Disallow notifications
  • Disallow priority display
  • Disallow status bar
  • Disallow banners
  • Disallow display on lock screen

You can do this process for all the other apps you are addicted to. If they bring no value in your life. It will change your life dramatically and instantly. 

If your phone turns off maybe because of the battery and the minute you turn it on. You get a lot of notification you are locked in your phone right away.
