What does it mean to love someone so much it hurts?

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date July 30, 2020
  • No Comments on What does it mean to love someone so much it hurts?

Loving someone so much can make you insecure. Love never hurts on its own, if you are in love you should feel loved, you must feel happy and safe.

Once it hurts then it means it is no longer love. It can be depression, insecurity, or feeling neglected.

This is lovesickness. When you have lovesickness, it is the pain that can produce negative feelings. When you are in love. Especially if a person you are in love, with is not in front of you. This can also cause a heartbreak.  

Most people, if they love someone so much it hurt, they rarely think it a disease. They believe this is part of being in love, while this can be true it is not always the case.

Some medical professor and in poetry sometimes they refer to love someone as madness. Passionate love will always fade, this can cause depression or sadness. Most of the time if a relationship is still new. Partners enter the honeymoon phase and within a year or two years it busts itself out. When the honeymoon phase fades that when most feel the lovesickness.

This is an expression. It can also mean we care for that person. When a person is in love, we must take them seriously. While this can be a pleasant thing, it can also be dangerous.

List of common symptoms of lovesickness:

  • Extreme high level of mood or inflated self-esteem than normal
  • Depression, hopelessness, or helplessness
  • Nausea
  • Tearfulness
  • Insomnia, which may lead to fatigue
  • Lack of concentration
  • Loss of appetite or overeating
  • Stress, high blood pressure, pain in the chest, and heart. Acute insomnia
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder. Preoccupation and hoarding valueless but resonant items
  • Upset stomach, a change in appetite, dizziness, and confusion
  • Chronic neck pain, body tremors, intrusive thoughts, frequent flashbacks.
  • Rapid mood swings

Love addiction

Love addiction can also be a second cause loving someone can hurt you. If you spend every minute with your partner and for any reason. You cannot do that for a day it can make you feel sad. If you are in love, you can get addicted to several reasons like sex, silly talk, and more. You feel joys depend on your partner and if you cannot be with them, it hurts.

Bonding and having romantic love with emotions such as affection and trust. Can also make you deeply addicted to someone and the fear of losing them can hurt you. Some people don’t experience love growing up and they finally get that someone special in their adulthood, they can’t stand the thought of losing them. 

Unconditional love is rare to find. Love where everything thing is complete and no one is trying to change anyone, love that has no bounds. But some of us are lucky to experience such love, and if that happens we never want that feeling to go away.

Sometimes that not how love works. Love has difficult times, so you can’t always feel love. There is a famous quote that says “those with an enormous heart, get hurt the most” which is obvious. If you love someone deeply, you must experience sad feelings several times.

Selfishness

If you are in love, and you do everything, you could to prove to your partner you love them. But no matter what you do, they seem to not notice your effort. This can hurt you. What breaks off friendships? It is envy and selfishness.

The same with the relationship. If you love your partner deeply, but they only care for themselves. It a shame and you have a right to feel pain. Lack of empathy is the root of selfishness. A happy relationship is when partners share everything. Both partners loving one another the same way. 

Worry

If you love someone you will worry about them, and you can not run away from it. Thoughts, images in our heads, emotions, and actions can make us worried about our loved ones.

Sometimes we worry because we have a negative memory of the past, we don’t want that to happen again. If dating there is a lot you worry about from health to finance and more. If you think about all these things and yet no solution. It can bring negative thought to your head causing you to feel pain. It’s a natural response to expected future problems.

Partners in a long-distance relationship turn to worry more. This is understandable, a long-distance relationship can be very hard. They come with a lot of optical and if you are truly in love.

You may worry a lot and there is nothing much you can do to change the situation. But in some long-distance, you are not even worried about losing your partner. Your partner being away from you can be the worst feeling.

I have been in long-distance relationships couple of times and I’m glad I Am not there anymore.

When they go through personal problems.

Someone you are dating will go throng personal problems. Some of which you can be able to help them with and some of which you may not. This can be one of them:-

  • Health problems
  • Family problems
  • Financial problems
  • Job loss
  • The death of a loved one
  • And more

If your partner is facing difficulties and you see them depressed, this can hurt. While you can be supportive and sympathetic, it not nice seeing them like that. It no surprise if someone you love experience problems they will affect you too.

Feeling neglected

Being neglected is common in most relationships and it very painful. This can sometimes be a sign something is wrong in a relationship.

You may feel invisible and intimacy is no longer there. If you notice you are no longer connected to your partner it hurts. The following video talks more about what it like being neglected and the cause for it.

My advice to you, if you are in a relationship and you feel neglected. Please try to talk to your partner communication is very important in a relationship.

The Demand for Recognition

Men love to be respected, and women love attention. If you don’t give your wife or girlfriend enough attention, it creates pain.

If you are in a relationship, try to recognize your partner. Every time he or she does something for you.

So they can feel loved and recognized. Once your partner, asking if you still love them, or do you miss them?

This shows they feel neglected and spend some time with them.

Depressed person

If you feel the pain, it doesn’t matter if you feel that pain because of love. You have depressing and you need to find out how to treat depression. There are many ways to overcome depression. You can even see a doctor for professional help.

Your pain can be the side effects of depression. So you must fight it now. Healthline gives you 5 Tips for Dating with Depression. You should read, maybe it can help in your situation.

Being loved

Being loved is nice, but sometimes it is hard to love without problems. To avoid the pain you must love peacefully and doubtless.

Stop thinking about what can go wrong with you and your partner. Learn to trust each other and believe nothing wrong is going to happen.

Avoid over-thinking about your partner, if you find yourself thinking about him or her all the time. Try to change those thoughts and think about what can I do to improve this relationship.

You can read Tips for keeping the family happy. This will help you in focusing on the good side and have a positive attitude about your relationship.

Romance Disappointment

The book Why Love Hurts: A Sociological Explanation by Eva Illouz. Talks about from romantic fantasy to disappointment.

Romance drives the relationship. If there was a lot of romance in the honeymoon phase. This can make you feel connected to your partner.

What happens when romance stops? that when you might feel more pain. This is common in most girls they like to be touched in a certain way.

Even when they make love they like it done it a certain way. If you fail they get disappointed.

That does not mean they don’t love you anymore. They still love you but the romance side disappoints them and that can create love pain.
