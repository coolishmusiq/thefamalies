Everything you need to know about exes

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 27, 2020
  • No Comments on Everything you need to know about exes

Once you become an Ex, a lot of drama and confusion is involved. Should you block him? Should you continue supporting him? If I do what this means?

Today I’m going to help you deal with your ex the right way. Also, answer the question like. How do you know when it over? Do you still love your ex or you are just lonely?

There are many reasons you think about your ex.  You might miss those late-night talk and if you haven’t found a new partner. You will miss your ex the most. Thinking about your ex doesn’t mean you must get back together. Sometimes you must move on. Because going back might hurt you even more.

Accepting the relationship is over can be hard and the longer it takes, the harder it gets. After getting a new partner, it gets better. The thoughts of your ex will never be permanently forgotten. However, you will feel better after a few years. It like the death of someone we love. It gets hard, at the 1st until we learn to leave without them.

If you think about your ex. Yet you don’t think they deserve a second chance. It helps to spend more time with family and friends. Until you find someone.

Failing to find a new relationship will make you think about your ex even more. Don’t be stuck in your past, because it would be all you see. You can still find someone new. That will value and respect you.

There is a reason your last relationship ended. Don’t forget about those reasons. History always repeats itself, if we keep going back to your past.

If we are in a relationship, we follow certain patterns. Sometimes we miss things we did with our exes, not necessarily them.  If you are lucky to find someone new, who will teach you new patterns. That when we forget the old ones. 

Missing your ex means you are lonely, and you haven’t found a new life. Which will close the gap for your old life.

It is hard to understand. If you miss your ex or just having someone to spend quality time with. Or you just miss sex.

However, it also possible you are still in love with your ex. If you made a quick decision without thinking it through. Sometimes we end relationships just to avoid solving problems.

If that the case, your mind knows you ended the relationship without good reasoning. Your relationship can still work, you just need to put pride aside and solve your problems.

But there must never be a day when you try so hard to get your ex back. Doing this will give your ex more power and they might take you for granted. Even when you are truly in love with your ex. Wait until they prove they still have the attention in a relationship.

What do you do if you still like your ex?

Some relationships can end while you are still in love. If this happens, it can be hard to move on. Not because you cannot move on. You don’t want to move on. Maybe you still wish your ex can come back and apologize.

This can make you feel stuck in your past relationship. The worse thing that can happen, your ex might never apologize. If you still love you ex, yet no chance of a rebound. You must first accept it over. If you don’t accept it ended. You won’t think about what to do next.

You will keep thinking about what if you did things differently. Maybe the outcome would be different. Which is true, but you didn’t accept it. It takes two people to be in a relationship. If one is no longer be part of it, then it no longer a relationship.

The only time I can advise you to go after your ex, is when your ex haven’t moved on too. They are not involved in a new relationship and they are still in contact you. 

But going after your ex when they have moved on. Can put you in a very painful position. You can also talk to your friends and family members. People who knew your relationship very well. Ask for advice on what to do. If they know the relationship is not good for you, they will tell you.

Loving yourself and opening up to a new life. Will help you move your focus away from your ex. Make your breakup the opportunity to do new things. You couldn’t do it in a relationship.

When the breakup is still new. You will think about all the bad things about your ex. When time goes by you think about the wonderful memories. That when you will miss your ex the more. But just because you are thinking about the good times. Doesn’t mean your partner is good for you.

Going back to your ex just because you have the old wonderful memories. Does not mean all those good things will happen again if you get back together.

Why do exes contact you out of the blue?

Should I call back my ex? Your ex can contact you for different reasons. The best way to understand is to talk to them. Even though you can ignore him/her. Maybe you want peace in your life.

Some exes are lonely and want to have a conversation. Sometimes when they go through a hard time. They want support from you. Especially if they haven’t found a new partner to replace you.

It is totally up to you to answer or call back your ex. If you still have feeling and the reason for a breakup are not that bad. Why not! But if you feel they don’t deserve your time, then don’t give them attention.

Does dreaming of an ex mean they miss you?

Dreaming about your ex will make you question yourself. Do I still love my ex? If you have a new relationship. It will feel you are betraying your new partner. 

Dreaming about your ex can mean two things. You miss your ex or your ex misses you. Most cases if you thought about your ex before you went to sleep. There is a high chance you might dream of your ex that night.

If your ex thinks or talks about you. The universe can hear that and talk to you back with a dream.  This is why we dream about most recent events more often.

Dreaming about your ex giving you advice. Your mind is telling not to repeat same mistakes you did with your ex. 

Dreaming of your ex death doesn’t mean your ex is dying. But your feelings for the old relationship are dying.    

Can exes fall back in love?

Yes, your ex can fall back in love with you and it happens more than you might think.

If they lost no dignity in your breakup. It is still possible to get back together and still possible your ex misses you too.

This will also depend on the age of your relationship. If you and your ex have a lot of history together. It won’t be easy to move on for any of you.

But your relationship ended for a reason don’t forget. If your relationship with your ex was in an excellent position or healthy. It wouldn’t have ended in the 1st place.

Before you make your ex fall back in love. You must think of every problem you were having in your relationship. Think if they can be solvable or not. If your relationship had serious problems like cheating and abuse. Forget about that relationship, just forget.

But if your ex was bringing value in your life. Yet, you didn’t realize how much your ex was helping you. Now that your relationship is over. Your life is not complete anymore.

Think of everything you did which made you ex to fall out. Fix your self 1st before you go back to apologize. The only way to make your ex fall back. Will begin by you admitting all your mistakes and making sure you don’t repeat them.  

Exes often come back but don’t wait for your ex. They might not come back.

How do you know if an ex misses you?

How do you know if your ex secretly misses you? Most of us will use a breakup as a time to heal. Once we heal, we may miss our old relationship.

But even when we miss our exes, sometimes pride can kick in. We wait for them to miss us too.

If one of these things happens. Then your ex could be missing you.

  • If your ex blocked you on a breakup and recently unblocked you.
  • If your ex finds random excuses to contact you.
  • Start asking about you in your friends and family.
  • Call you or text you when they are drunk.
  • Start stalking you on social media.
  • Your ex is always happy to see you.
  • Remembers your birthdays.
  • Give your kids some money.

These can be clues that your ex is missing you. But do you miss your ex? Your ex can miss you, yet if you have moved on. Don’t go back.

Do exes come back after months?

Most of the time they will. Removing someone in your mind is difficult. Some forget after a few months, for some, it gets harder.  Sometimes we have 0 control of our memories.

Most of the time your ex will come back after seeing you dating someone else. Seeing you with someone else can make your ex jealous.  If you are in a new relationship. They feel like someone is winning something that belongs to them.

But if your ex date someone else quickly after a breakup. They might not come back. The only time they will come back is when. This new relationship they started is not what they hoped for.

If they also celebrated the breakup publicly. Even when they miss you, the chances of them coming back are too small.

Sometimes your ex will get back to you, just to prove a point. Or if you used to support your ex. They might miss you when they face tough times.

If your ex comes back after months. It can be a good thing or he/she is coming back with more problems.

Should you keep supporting your ex?

Stop supporting your ex after a breakup. Just stop! If you are no longer in a relationship. That person is no longer your responsibility.

Whether you support your ex emotional or financial. It is not your place to do that. Let your ex go, either they are with you or they are not.

Stop trying to make yourself look important. You are just forcing yourself in a place you don’t belong to.

Even if your ex is the one coming back for support. Keep in mind, they deserve nothing. They left you!

Does silence make an ex miss you?

Silence can make a man miss you. But not all men!

Silence can also make a man move on, from his past relationship.

Keep in mind, silence can work but there are no guarantees. A lot can happen while you are silent. You also have no right to blame him, since you went silent.

But it always a good idea to lay low sometimes. This helps you avoid saying things you don’t mean. Just go silent make your ex wonder why you don’t give them attention anymore. Have you found someone or you really don’t have a feeling for them anymore?

Silence will make your exes realize your absence and start missing. But this can also trigger him to look for a new partner. Since your exes are now feeling lonely.

You can cut a guy off and let him miss you. Don’t give him too much space though if you still love him. Because he will develop new patterns without you.

If you want to stop talking to your ex. So, your ex can miss you, know it does not end there. You still need to do more to make sure your plan works. Try this

  • Keep in contact with your ex family members. Even though you don’t talk to your ex.
  • Don’t block your ex on social media. Make your ex see everything you are up too.
  • Invest in yourself, go out, and have fun.

These can help if you want your ex to miss you. Yet you don’t want to talk to him yet. 

Can you stay a friend with an ex?

People ask this question a lot. The reason they want to stay friends with their ex. They still care about them and enjoy their company.

If you also feel this way, I have a question for you. If you still enjoy each other’s company, why did you break up?

Why didn’t you both fix your problem instead of breaking up?

Because if you breakup with someone. It means you want nothing to do with them. But this is not a simple question to answer. I think it can really depend on you and what you want.

Someone who cheated on you, can not expect you to be friends with them after a breakup. However, if the breakup was just a small miss understanding. It’s understandable to remain friends. It still depends on how big are your problems and can they be fixed.

But if you or your ex have new partners. Staying friends is a no go!

Since the relationship end for a reason. Staying a friend with your ex can also take away your time to heal. It is also a disrespect to your new relationship.

Why do guys act like they don’t care after a breakup?

Sometimes they are not acting, they really don’t care. If you care about someone you do everything to keep them. If you still act like you don’t care about a breakup. Then you didn’t deserve that relationship in the 1st place.

Stop following your ex if you see they don’t care after a breakup. Move back and force on you.

Once someone becomes your ex, they need not explain themselves to you. Because you are no longer together. That why they say what yours will always come to you.

If your ex doesn’t come back after a breakup. The relationship was never yours. Move on, you will find someone new. There is no person who is better than everyone. You can always find someone who is better than your ex. Don’t be stuck with people who treat you like you mean nothing to them.

Reason to take a break after a breakup

There are many reasons you should heal. After a failed relationship. Many of us jump into the next relationship. With hope, it will heal the past relationship. However, in most cases, it doesn’t. taking a break can be good for your mental health.

Jumping to the next relationship can replace pain with happiness. But it won’t heal you, once your new relationship slows down. You will feel more pain. You might even regret your decision. Going back is no longer a wise choice.

If your new relationship fails. You are now facing pain for two relationship. Which both didn’t work.

Not allowing yourself to heal can create a lot of problems. Like trust issues, anger, insecurities, etc.

Moving on without healing can negatively affect your emotions and the way you think.

Breakups can be a trauma no matter what side you are on.

Recovering after a breakup

Some break up can be bad. But you need to pick yourself up and move on. Your life doesn’t end in a breakup, there is still a lot you can accomplish.

Recovering also mean you finding the right partner the next time. Not repeating the same patterns of bad partners.

  • In the first step, focus on helping yourself. Getting professional help if you feel your need one, talking to your family members. Talking to your friends asking them what to do in your situation. Reaching out to people who recently recovered from relationship fail, and more. You know who are your good friends and best family members are. Because not everyone knows you and understands you.
  • Teach yourself that it is okay to feel pain after a breakup. The pain is not permanent, and it is not the end of your world. You are in control and you have the power to take yourself out of that pain.
  • Take some time and point out what did you do wrong. So, you can start learning from your mistakes and avoid them as early as possible in your next relationship. Learn what made you pick this person and what was good about him. What was bad about him or what was bad about you both in your past relationship? Makes sure you get all the fact right before you start a new relationship. I cannot guarantee your next relationship will work out. But you will avoid most problems from your previous relationship.
  • Don’t contact each other unless there are kids involved. But if you don’t have kids with your ex. There is no need to follow your ex on social media or keeping in touch. Because you will be starting noticing things your ex is doing right. Which can make you fall in love and forget why you guy broke up in the first place. 
  • The experiment has fun and put yourself out there. Try new things, try a new life. Make yourself available not only to a new partner. But new friends also. Everything new can be exciting, which can help you heal faster. The more you try new things, the faster you will move away from your past.
  • Regain confidence and trust in yourself. Just believe you can gain anything that you want. You need not settle for less and keep going back to people who hurt you. Trust yourself you can move on and get everything that you want. It is a process but when you know what you want. Eventual it will come to you.
  • Maintain your dignity after a breakup. Don’t be a person who keeps begging for a relationship. No need to always try to fix your relationship. Especially if you see your partner don’t care anymore. If someone makes up their decision that they no longer want to be in a relationship with you. Stop fighting it and accept their decision. Begging to make you look desperate, which makes your partner lose attraction even more. You are going to be okay regardless of what is happening in your life. 

How do you tell she’s not over her ex?

Dating someone who is not over his ex is painful. But how can you tell if your new girlfriend is not over her ex?

These can be signs she is not over her ex.

  • She keeps making excuses to visit you.
  • She talks about her past relationship a lot.
  • She still keeps in contact with her ex.
  • She gets angry at you for no reason.
  • Never enjoy sex with you.
  • Always treating you, she will leave the relationship.
  • She doesn’t want to meet your family.

Should you take back an ex who dumped you?

If you still love them you can, but if you have moved on don’t.

Because this means whatever they thought they were doing didn’t work. Now they are coming back to you as a backup plane.

You need someone who will be full committed to you.

Not someone who will come and go when it suits him/her. 

Who hurts more after a breakup?

Women are more sensitive than man. They also sacrifice a lot in a relationship then most men. They even sacrifice their family just to be with you.

Very few men can move out of their home just to be with a woman.

If a woman enters a relationship. They enter with everything they have.

If the relationship doesn’t work, it is safe to say. Women will hurt the most.

Who moves on faster after a breakup?

Man can move on before a breakup. By the time the relationship gets in to a break up they are long gone. Sometimes by the time they end the relationship. They are already involved a new one.

While women on the other hand. Needs some time to heal after a break. It can take them a very long time to heal.  Sometimes it even takes years for a woman to move on.

The only time man will have a hard time moving on after a breakup. Will only be a time where he fails to find a better partner. Which will replace the old one.

Keep in mind, even though men move on faster. This does not mean they don’t miss you, or they are not hurting over the past relationship. They do but they don’t allow the pain to control them.

Why people get stuck after breakup?

People sometime get stuck with they ex. Even though they know the relationship was not good for them.

These can be the reasons they are stuck.

  • They get in denial, they know relationship was not good for them. Yet they keep think it can be fixed. But they failed to fix it.
  • Thinking their happiness belong to their ex-partner. That without their ex, they can’t have a good time.
  • Hold on to the old promises.
  • Focusing too much on the great time they both shared. Not willing to try new things.
  • Busy asking themselves why me, instead of asking. What did I learn from this?
  • Comparing their life to someone else’s life.
  • Staying in contact with their ex.

Conclusion

Most of my advice are based on my past experience. However, how you treat your ex is total up to you.

But if your keep making the wrong decisions there will be consequence. Those results can make you hurt repeatedly.

If you find yourself hurting by a same person repeatedly. This means you must look back into your decisions. Try to understand what role are you playing. How can you get yourself out?

Don’t allow more pain in your life. If it doesn’t work, try new things that works. Stop trying things that can’t be fixed and love yourself. Because you deserve all the good things like everyone else.

What are the worst mistakes parents can make?

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 16, 2020
  • No Comments on What are the worst mistakes parents can make?

The mistake we do as parents is to think we can be perfect parent. When there is no such thing as perfect parenting. Because you are older, doesn’t mean you are always right. Some days you will say wrong things to your kids or be hash. Nothing wrong with going back to your kids and apologize.  You must never have the pride to apologise. Not only with your kids, but in life.

Learn more about how to apologise and when.

Every parent makes mistakes, and every kid makes mistakes. You will make mistakes there is no way to prevent them. You can only reduce and cut your mistakes. But you can’t prevent them.

Beliefs we have can make us parent the wrong way. We must be open to learning new things and changed as the world change. Trying to raise your kids the same way you grew up won’t work. If your parents punished you a lot as a kid. Which made a powerful man. Don’t think it is wise to raise your kids that way. Beliefs are what will make us do most mistakes.

If you have a certain style of parenting and you see it not working. Do not force it, learn something else. Notice everything that is not working. Drop your ego, understand there is so much you don’t know.

let look at what are the worst mistakes parents can make.

Not choosing your words wisely.

Words are powerful, and they can build or destroy. You must be careful about what you say to your children. My mom passed away when I was very young, and there are things my stepmother said to me. When I was 9 years old, about my bio mother. Which I carry to this day. Because they were mean things to say to a child.

Words as parents sometimes can come out the wrong way. Words have a deep meaning. They can heal and be weapons.

Talking too much with your kids.

Avoid talking too much with your kids. Say things you want to say and give them time to process everything. If you spend 30 minutes talking to your kids. They won’t remember everything, only the thing which makes sense to them. You don’t have to say the same things over and over for kids to understand you. If you do that it feels like abuse they heard the 1st time. Yet you still repeating it over and over.

.Less is more! The worst mistakes parents make when talking to kids. Not paying attention to a kid’s needs.

Not realizing how expensive babies can be.

You are going to be the one carrying for your baby. Know your financial status and understand if you are ready. If you have over one kid. They can get too expensive and if you have no plan. You will experience problems. You might even be angry at kids for destroying your budget. While it is you who had no birth plan.

With babies, we don’t know exactly when are they coming. But we need to save as early as possible. So, when that day comes, we are ready. It is difficult to save after you have a baby. It much easier if you save before you have a child.

Neglecting kids’ hunger

You can’t expect your kids to be at their best while their tummy is empty. Hunger makes it hard to concentrate and can escalate misbehaving. Even us as parents we are likely to be impatient when we are hungry.  Parents sometimes force kids to go out and play with other kids. Therefore, they can avoid making food for them. If you do that stop it now, it is not good for your child.

It your job to make sure your kids eat at all times. If their appetite drop take that seriously.

Giving your kids Alcohol

Parents have a tendency of making kids taste alcohol. When a child start acts odd. they think it hilarious and take videos to post on social media. The following tweet is the great example of bad parenting.

I have watched this 35 times pic.twitter.com/QNusyBCYG0

You must choose your kids over alcohol. If you allow your kids to taste alcohol. You are the worst parent and they must arrest you. The drinking age is 18 years old here in South Africa, and in some parts of the world, it is 21 years old. There is a reason for this.

I see this a lot, even in my family. I never understand. How can you do such a thing to your child? What lessons are you giving them? I just don’t get it!

There is a lot you can do with your kids. Things like helping them learn to share. Instead of giving them alcohol.

Shouting at your kids

You must avoid freaking out for your kids. Shouting undermines your ability to get your words through. They either shut down or get mad in response to your shouting. Shouting at your kids also makes them feel you hate them. They also feel like the worst kids and hate themselves. Avoid shouting!

Try to talk some sense with them without rising your voice.

Blocking your kids from technology

There is a debate about smartphones and laptops. Parents have a fear of buying smartphones for their children. They might play games all the time and stop doing school work. Or watch unsuitable content for their age and more. This is all true!

But let stop focusing on what can go wrong. Think about what technology can bring to your child. How I usually do this, when I’m thinking about buying a smartphone for my child. Yet, I’m worried they might use it the wrong way.

First, I buy a new phone and give a child the old one. When I give my child the old one, I never tell them it theirs. “This is my old phone, you can use it. Because I have a new one. But if I see this phone is making you lose focus on important things like school. I will take it away.”

While I know the phone is no longer mine. Yet I don’t let the kid know that. So, they can give it some respect.

Same thing with laptops. I never give them 100% ownership. But the worst thing you can do is to block your children from using technology. I try to teach my kids everything that can help them be independent. Like how to invest in the stock market. Blogging and everything that they can start as a hobby. But in a log run generate good income.  Would it be nice to see your kids making their 1st $1000 at 16? Wouldn’t that be great?

Shaming your kids

Shaming kids is another worst mistake parent do. If your child did something wrong. There is no need to compare your child with the one that is doing great. By doing this, and it will make your kids hate one another. Because you make them think. The other one is bad, and the other is the best. Parents also expect kids to do things the way their parents taught them. If they fail, then they see them as losers. Stop shaming your kids, it is wrong. Give them time to make mistakes and learn their own mistake.

The following video help us with ways we unknowingly shame our children.

Parents fighting with kids

We see this almost everywhere. When after parents broke up, they fighting using kids. Kids have nothing to do with our breakups. Kids deserve both parents, even when those parents are no longer together. A child must have a relationship with both parents.

I experienced this a lot with my 1st child’s mother. She would try to get back to me and if that didn’t work out. She would cut me out completely, to a point where it would be impossible to contact both. That was painful to me, and I’m sure it was for my daughter.

Parents need to stop involving kids in their personal problems. Love your children more than you dislike each other.

Don’t make the mistake to not be the part of your child growing up.

Be a leading example to your kids

I think I have talked about this before in one of my posts. How kids can be copycats. You need to be a leading example in everything you want them to be. If you want them to treat everyone with respect. You must be the one treating your family with respect first. Kids learn more when they watch you doing something. Then when they listen to you talking about it. 

If you want more kindness in the world, put some there.

Not teaching your kids boundaries

Families must have boundaries and kids too. You must train children to do a certain thing and to act in certain ways. Example, your kids must knock every time they enter your bedroom. If you don’t teach them this as early as possible. One day they will see things you don’t want them to see and they will humble you.

Setting restrictions teach our kids to respect and know their limits.

Never be your child’s best friend

Kids need to understand you are not a friend. But a parent, if they have problems. They must come to you. If you have problems, you won’t come to them, but their father. It is very rare that you will tell them your secrets. However, it is not okay for them to keep a secret from you. They are not allowed to use your things without your person.

Long story short, we can be close. But not too close to a point where there are no boundaries.

Expecting your child to fulfill your dreams

We all have things we wanted to do growing up. Which we didn’t, no need to force our kids to do things we failed to do ourselves. You can try to teach them if you feel is important and can help them. However, if you see, they are not into it. Don’t force them, let them pick their own system of life.

Why marriages is not an easy task

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 15, 2020
  • No Comments on Why marriages is not an easy task

Talk about family problems you can expect in any marriage. Every family has problems and we can never run away from it. If you believe your family won’t have problems. You are just setting yourself for a disaster. Because you won’t know how to act. When you reach that point in life.

If your family doesn’t have problems it because you are the problem and they haven’t told you.

  • Either you are going to be a problem
  • You are going to marry a problem
  • Either you are going to marry a family with problems
  • Or you will have problems entering your marriage from the outside
  • Either your family is the problem 
  • Or the combination of them all

“Marriage will show you how selfish you are, and parenting will show you how angry you are.” That true and common in marriages.

Children are growing and still have lots of mistakes to make. Parents want them to act right all the time.

The wife is raised by a different family and the husband. There are days where they won’t see eye to eye. Some days one will be selfish and the other will have pride.

If pride enters a marriage. Your spouse won’t care if his/her beliefs are wrong or right. They know what they want and will try to convince you to do things their way.

Some days you will wish they can do things in a certain way. But no one will care about you.

We need to be aware all these struggles will happen. How you all deal with those problems. Will determine if your marriage will work or fail.  

There is no such thing as a perfect marriage. Only families with better problem-solving skills.

If they do things your way all the time. You are selfish, maybe your partner won’t tell you. But that selfishness and not the right way to treat your family.

Both partners must have a voice and play a part in how we arrive in conclusions. You must include your partner in your decisions. You must talk it through and come out with what seems to be a win for both of you.

Why it so hard to forgive your spouse

If your spouse upsets or lets you down. It is difficult to forgive. If you forgive someone, it means you understand what they did to you. But you will no longer hold it against them.

This process is not as easy as it sounds. Your spouse must help you in forgiving him or her. If they are not sorry for what they did or defensive about it. Forgiving become very hard.

It gets easy to forgive your spouse. If they sit down with you and talk about the problem. Telling you they understand what they did was wrong. They are sorry and it won’t happen again. They completely understand there is nothing they can do to make this right. Except for saying sorry and they feel bad while take a responsibility for it. Last, they feel bad that it hurt you.

That helping your spouse forgive you and it make it easy. Once your spouse understands that you know what you did was wrong. They can forgive you and let go.

What will make this hard is when your spouse says “I didn’t do something wrong, what your problem? Just get over it”. This makes you even more upset because they did something wrong and they are not sorry about it.

Your spouse is not taking responsibility and they make you sound crazy for even talking about it. This makes it hard for a marriage to be healthy and moving forward.

This becomes a problem if your spouse hurts you over and over. Without being sorry because it keeps adding up. Even if they always come around and ask for forgiveness. It raises a question if you love me the way you say you do. Why do you hurt me in the 1st place? It’s hard to forgive someone who constantly hurt you.

So, until there is a change in their behavior it never easy.

Cheating in marriages

Cheating is the worst issue in marriage and it is hard to recover from it.  We see it happening more and more these days. If someone cheats, they can’t blame anything or anyone. Rather than blaming themselves.

Making a big deal out of the small stuff

I and my wife were talking about the other last night. It seems like people like to make a big deal out of the small stuff.

She used to be like this, and there was a time where I was like this. But as you grow older, you realize this not how you live with others. We all make mistakes, there is no need to fight about everything.

Because having a tendency to make a big deal out of minor matters can ruin a relationship. I always tell people not to start a fight unless it beyond your powers.

If you make big deal out of everything. That when your partner might say you talk too much. This can be the beginning of an unhealthy relationship.

So, today I’m saying, Stop Making A Big Deal Over the Little Things.

Find good habits for your relationship. This is the nasty habit and you must find a way to of breaking this habit. It does more harm than good.

There are many good patterns you can teach yourself. Same as bad ones, if you want a healthy relationship. You must stop the bad patterns.

If you make a big deal out of every minor situation. This means you controlling, and no one enjoys being with a controlling partner.

Once you stop making a big deal out of everything. This is when you will learn the skill to solve problems, without fighting with your partner.

Sometimes you need to know your partner is old enough to know right for wrong.  No need to remind him no, this is wrong every time.

He already knows what he did was wrong. 

Always ask yourself is this worth my time. It is easy to shout at people until we lose them.

I understand it can happen, that your partner thinks it not a big deal. But it is for you. That also happens all the time.

In a case, where your partner thinks it not a big deal and you think otherwise. Talk to them like the adult. They would understand no need to fight every time.

If you have talked to your partner several times and he keeps doing the same. Then we can make it a big deal.!

Preventing fighting from ruining your relationship

The first thing which is wrong. We think relationship and marriages are hard.

So, when our relationship has problems. We take this as normal and don’t see it as a problem. So, we can deal with it.

We get advice. Relationships are hard work. If you want to stay in a relationship, fight to keep it.

But you don’t fight for a relationship, which is health. If you must always fight for your relationship, that not health. Some day you might realize no, I can’t take it anymore.

The problem started when you believed fight is normal in a relationship. A single fight can be normal. But if it happens often, that not normal.

You will have arguments, issues, and hard times. But should not escalate to fights. Because in a good relationship there is clear communication and problem-solving strategy.

Consistent fighting and making a big deal out of small things. That not normal and it could be your strategy of dealing with fight is not working. It can also mean one of you is falling out of love.

  • Falling out of love can make you fight for things that don’t matter.
  • It can also mean you are angry about something. That you haven’t addressed yet. For example, they do not please you in bed. So everything upsets you.
  • You could fight about something that happened in the past. You have talked about it and it passed. But months later you re-think it and be angry. This time you show your anger in other things. Even though it is still connected to what happened in the past.

This can make your relationship unhealthy, and you might not even realize it. If this continues it creates a bigger problem. Can even end the relationship.

Break the circle of fighting

Deal with what in front of you right now.

What I mean is, don’t talk about what happened yesterday or the other month. Instead, fix only what you are talking about now.

If you had a problem and talked about it. Then your partner apologized. If he apologized and you saw, he meant what he said. Never bring that up again. Even when you are fighting about something else.

If it is something that happened last week. It must be completely off the table. You fix everything as it happens and moving on with your relationship. Without allowing problems to add up on top on one another.

Keeping a record of everything is wrong. First, you did this second, you did that, now you are doing this. That all wrong!

Because you have talked about it and came up with a solution. Why are you even mad about it for a week or a month later?

When you keep bringing up things. Which happened in the past, over and over. You are not allowing your relationship to move forward.

When you focus at the moment. Your communication skills will improve.

If you keep everything that happened in the past. You will win the fight. But your partner knows what you did in the past, too. So, if he is also bringing everything too, he will win. This means the conversation is not going forward and the relationship.

If you forgive someone it means you put that in the past. If it too much to forgive don’t continue with that relationship.

Words you need to avoid when arguing in the relationship.

Let me assume the reason for you to read this article is that you are in a similar situation. Where you and your partner are always fighting and one is making a big deal out of everything.

I want to warn you about two words you should avoid when fighting. These words are 1 always 2 never!

It is something you never thought about before. But it is very damaging.

It can take some time to break the habit. Knowing this is something I should stop will make you one step ahead.

Let me make an example of what I mean.

  • You are always starting a fight
  • You always messing things up
  • We always do what you want us to do
  • You always make fun of me
  • I always must do everything
  • You always ignore me
  • You never listen to me
  • You never cared
  • You never help
  • You are never romantic
  • You never put effort into anything
  • You never let me choose

Think about it. The chances of never and always being accurate are small to none. We use always and never when we frustrated or angry about something.

What these words do. They also make your partner frustrated too. Because even though your partner was wrong. Your partner won’t agree with what you are saying. This can start a separate fight on top of the one. You are already fighting about.

Two, it puts extra blame on your partner. For example, if you say your partner always mess thing up. This means since the day you meant them. They always mess thing up and that such a wrong thing to say.It not fair to say because it isn’t true.

Get to the root of the problem. Solve that problem without involving everything that happened before.

If you don’t get to the root of the problem. You leave that problem unsolved. Because instead of solving it. You brought back the old one. Even those were not solved. Because you also brought the older ones.

This becomes a chain of unsolved problems, and they will keep coming up. Causing you to fight all the time!

This can make you make a big deal out of small things. Because a lot of problems are stuck in your head unsolved.
Let us say your partner is talking about something you do that they don’t like. But they are not fighting with you yet. Telling you what they don’t like.

If you respond by saying, you always picking a fight. This means you don’t care about their feelings and you are reversing the problem to them.

The next thing that is going to happen. The fight will worsen. The other partner may even become more frustrated. Because you show you don’t even care.

So, this way of handling things solves no problem. It makes things worse!

If your partner use always or never in you. What then?

There will be a time where your partner says you always do this, or you never do this. You as a person who already knows. How damaging those words can be. You must have a different manner.

If your partner says. You always pick a fight. You must keep calm and say come sit down here, let talk about it.

I’m sorry you feel that way. That I always pick a fight. I know we need to be better in handling our problems. Show your partner you care about their feelings.

Ask them what is it moving forward that they want you to do. To solve this problem.

You are solving a problem. Instead of reversing the problem to the other partner or bringing up the past.

Understand what exactly is bordering your partner right now. What role can I play moving forward to make things better for us? How can we make this better together?

If you talk to your partner like this. They feel

  • You are hearing
  • You respect their feelings
  • You care
  • You are working together as a team.

By doing this, it can cool the other partner way faster. Showing you care, makes the other person feel loved and acknowledged. This is why you are together.

Your partner doesn’t want to be angry. They are not speaking to you to annoy you. They are trying to solve the problem. So don’t make the problem worse.

If you tell your partner “you never listen to me”. This means they have never listened to you, ever since you started dating. How true is that?

It like you are telling your partner, they are always wrong and the only reason you are still together is you. Which is not fair!

Each person has a unique mind and voice. Using always and never will make your relationship to grow apart. This will cause a lot of unnecessary fights. Without you understanding where they have come from. 

Tips for fighting fair

This article is about a relationship. Where partners fight all the time. The relationship where everything turns in to a big deal. The aim is to help you reduce fight in your relationship.  Because fight can turn even a perfect relationship into a sour one.

So, we need to look at this problem in many viewpoints and give you unique solutions.

There are many ways partners deal with the conflict. Both partners must not try to win the conversation. Without interest in solving the problem.

Both partners want the fight to end. But it cannot if the fight is going in the wrong direction.  

If there is a problem in your relationship. But both of you are defensive. It is less likely you will move forward.  

How do you fight fair?

  1. There is a saying don’t go to bed angry. It’s time we rethink that. We must teach our self to take a break if something is frustrating us. Even if that means going to bed angry, it is fine we will talk about it tomorrow. But this will help us avoid saying things we don’t mean because we are angry. It also gives us time to think. The next day you might even realize. I don’t want to talk about this anymore, it is not worth the time. This will help us stop saying things in a mean way, harsh and hurtful. Saying things that can’t be forgetting.
  2. The second method of fighting fair is the one we talk about early to stop using the words always and never. Because they are not accurate, and they do more harm than good.
  3. Find a solution. Stop fighting back your partner. Work together so you can find a solution.
  4. Focus on the present. Forget about the past wrongs.
  5. Never interrupt your partner. Let your partner finish and give your viewpoint.
  6. Apologize if needed.
  7. Never fight in front of your kids. Children a copycat, if you fight opposite to them. They might fight the other kids at school. You might blame them. But it is you who are teaching them the wrong things.
  8. Watch your words.

Conclusion

If your relationship is always boiling and you fight all the time. Learn to listen to your partner. Forget who is wrong for a moment and focus on how we can solve this.

Be loving to each other. Find the strategy to solve problems in your relationship. But you can’t come up with a strategy during fights.

Coming with a strategy will need both partners to be cool and claim. Talk about your bad habits that are costing a relationship. When you are both in the right space.

Communication is the key, talk to your partner, and a find solution.
