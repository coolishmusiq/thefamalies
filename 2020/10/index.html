Why people are losing interest in dating apps

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date October 26, 2020
  • No Comments on Why people are losing interest in dating apps

Most dating apps have the worst reviews, and it is not surprising. I have tried dating online myself and it was a waste of my time. Dating apps back in 5-10 years used to be great. They were not this popular. Thus, you might hear stories like I meet my wife or husband on a dating app. This can sound tempting. The online dating game has changed a lot over the years. It is risky and a waste of time. Let look at the major reasons most people are losing interest in online dating.

Premium and gold members are a scam in dating apps

Most online dating platforms will force you to get premium or gold membership. But the wrong thing about this. Even with a paid version, they still limit you. When you are sending too many inboxes with your premium or gold subscriptions. They will tell you have reached your daily limit. Which means you must pay more or wait 24 hours to send more.

If you don’t have premium and gold subscriptions, it is worse.

  • They will hide who liked your profile
  • You won’t see who views your profile
  • Limit the number of profiles you can like
  • The number of inboxes you can send is not enough
  • Some won’t allow you to talk with gold or premium members. If you use a free version.

Scammers in online dating are real

Scam in dating apps has raised a lot of questions. You get young players and the enormous ones. Common scams can include the following :

  1. Catfishing! This is when someone is pretending to be someone. Yet, using pictures they found on social media or Google. Therefore, you fell in love thinking you have found your match. Yet, you to find you are being played.
  2. Picture editing! We use a lot of editing software these days. Sometimes you meet someone online and they look super-hot. But when you meet them in person, you get disappointed.
  3. Money scams! Some will scam you small amounts. Like when someone says they will visit you. But they don’t have money for a bus fare. When you send a bus fare. They will block you right away. Others target rich people and they will stick around for a very long time. Maybe 6 to 12 months. Then make up stories and borrow serious cash. You fall for a trap and they disappear.
  4. Human trafficking! These are the most dangerous people in the online dating community. They also use fake accounts, fake pictures, and fake locations. You can’t tell someone is trafficking human. Until you meet them. Then no one will ever see you again. Which is sad. Yet it happens all the time.
  5. Junk ads! Most dating apps have shady ads and some have viruses. This can harm your phone or computer. If not, add you on subscriptions. That will steal your airtime every day. You might end up changing your sim card.
  6. Data breaches! They have accused some platforms of selling information from their clients. Some are not secured enough. Hackers can steal your personal information. Like numbers and emails to use them for personal reasons.

Dating apps can destroy your reputation

People who use these apps say it is embarrassing. It is hard to tell people you got someone online. If you see someone you know on a dating app. you can’t help it but wonder why are they using the dating apps. They also think the same of you. These apps are not for shy people like me.

Some use fake names and fake pictures. Not to scam people, but to hide from family and friend. After you have exchanged numbers. They reveal themselves to you. That if they like you.

You find people are in relationships or even married. But using these apps. which destroy their status, and the reputation of their spouse or whoever they are dating.

Users are addicted to dating platforms.

People go online to look for a partner and find one. But they never leave the dating apps. even when they found someone. They keep wanting more. Thinking they can get someone even better.

The most online relationship fails. They know they can always get more and more. The moment you break a promise or cannot show up. They are not interested in you anymore. Fixing problem is not the way they do things.

They want hookups not genuine relationship

If you are looking for a long-term relationship. Don’t waste your time with any dating app, try something else. Because they are looking for sex, not a proper relationship. If you think you will find someone who is loyal and committed. Don’t be surprised if it ends in tears.

You might get STD from a relationship which didn’t work.

The funny thing about this. You can meet someone online today and they only know 1 or 2 pictures of you. But within a few minutes, they want you to visit them. They don’t know your background. No clue about what you like and don’t like. Yet they are already talking about seeing each other. This is obvious they don’t care about you. It all about sex with them.

Dating apps are not for unemployed people

These apps have a lot of gold diggers in one place. Then any other online community. They can tell you are unemployed by looking at your pictures.

If they see you don’t have money. You won’t even get a reply. Some won’t even read your message. This why most get rejected on dating apps. you won’t have time to explain yourself. Once they visit your profile and see your picture. They judged you and there is nothing you can do.

If you want to survive, you must take your picture in high places. Your profile must interest enough.

If you are working, you must have a car. After you must take them out to pleasant restaurants. If you cannot afford these things. You might as well delete your app. 90% of them will reject you every day.

You don’t need a dating app.

Most realize they don’t need dating apps. you can find love in the old fashion way. Learn how you can stay happily single until you find someone. You may also like, things every girl should do before she says yes in a relationship.

Love is everywhere, you need to be available. If you stay home all the time. You must go out more. If you still using dating platforms. You are looking for your match in the wrong place. Putting your life at risk and wasting your time.

Respect yourself and delete your dating profiles.

Staying happily single while looking for someone

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date October 24, 2020
  • No Comments on Staying happily single while looking for someone

There is no way we can control. When the right person will come into our lives. But we can make ourselves visible for all the possibilities.

If you are looking for a partner. You must expand your life and be creative. Hopefully, on that journey, you will find a lover of your life.

Find things you can do and enjoy. But increasing your chances of finds a partner. Things you rarely do. Even though you have an interest in them.

Don’t be afraid to take an invitation from your friends. But avoid hanging out with the same people all the time. This will get boring. If you hang out with the same people you. It must be different locations at least.

It can be a challenge to go to places where you don’t know anyone. But that where new friendship and fun will come from.

Choices are unlimited you can try –

  • Gym
  • Visit coffee shops
  • Travel to new places
  • Visit jazz clubs
  • Church and more

Take the lead in trying new things

If no one is inviting you to try new things. Don’t sit and wait, invite them yourself.
Even if after all this you don’t find a partner. You will still make new friends and you will have a good time doing it. This is a win, win process.
Taking a lead will not always mean you have to invite friends to certain events. You can also volunteer for any job or service that you can enjoy.
Everyone can meet someone. We need to be active and date ready.

Loving your self first before finding love

  • Take yourself out
  • Buy yourself pleasant things
  • Celebrate yourself and more.

The more you celebrate and love yourself. More people will find you attractive. If you don’t love yourself how can you expect someone to love you?
Loving and taking care of yourself. Will give the first impression to most people you meet. This increases your chances of finding someone.
This makes you proud of who you are and you also get respect.

The right and the wrong places to get a lover

Let be realistic, not all the places. You go to and have fun will lead you to the right partner.
Think about people you can attract from each place. Because certain people attend certain places. Don’t forget about age.
For example: – if you love music and you would love to meet someone with the same interest. Attending music conferences nearby can be a good place to start. You will attract people with the same interest. While learning.

Common places to meet a partner while having fun

  • Soft club with low or no music. Where it not crowded and you can talk. Without being distracted by people and music.
  • The grocery store. Though there isn’t that much fun in grocery stores. You can get a brilliant partner while shopping. Dress up look nice and clean. Don’t but everything same day. Visiting different stores can increase your chances. Pick stores wisely shopping in town and buying in malls is not the same.
  • Visit art galleries nearby can also be a great experience.
  • National gardens are wonderful too. Most people go there to clear their heads and available to have some talk.

Look at yourself and understand your needs. Because there is a lot to do out there for different people.

Terrible places for meeting partners

Some places can be good for fun. But not for finding a partner.

  • Places like stadiums during soccer games can be fun. But it loud and hard to have a decent conversation. Very few can pay attention to you. Everyone is talking and focusing on a soccer match.
  • Night clubs, some say they can be a good place to meet friends. I disagree with them. They can be risky and might attract criminals. People who are attending clubs. These are the people who rarely do well with commitment and loyalty. You can get lucky, but it not worth your time.
  • Libraries will also waste your time. No one is interested in talking to anyone. They are there for studies. Some will even have a sign of no talking.

You still need to use your judgment and find what best for you.

Lower your dating expectations

I understand the entire purpose to put yourself out there is to meet someone. But lower your expectations because we don’t know. If you will find your partner this week, next month, or in a year.
If you have high expectations and you don’t find a partner soon. This can make you give up too early. It is about timing.
Put yourself out there and trust in God. Don’t forget to keep it safe and protect yourself from COVID-19. Have fun God will do the rest.

Should you make your kids apologize?

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date October 23, 2020
  • No Comments on Should you make your kids apologize?

It a great practice to teach our kids to apologize. When our kids say I’m sorry they should mean it. We shouldn’t force our kids to apologize for the things they are not sorry for. But we must teach them to apologize and teach them not to over apologies.

Before we talk much about this one thing to remember. How your kids behave also affects your reputation. If your kids provoke people and don’t feel sorry for their actions. This says a lot about you as a parent.

Kids don’t like to be wrong and when they are wrong. They don’t like to admit they are wrong. If they must apologize, they try to fight it first or blame the other person. There is a lot to learn about the power of the apology.

Forcing kids to say I’m sorry. You can increase anger and shame. This also makes them say sorry all the time without meaning it or changing their behavior.

When to apologizing for your kids ‘

Though I don’t enjoy apologizing for my kids. Sometimes kids can leave us with no choice. Especially if the problem involves someone older than your child. Also, when your child broke something and it needs to be paid for.

This will force you to act and take a responsibility for your child. Then later you can deal with your child. But there is no need to fight with your child. You must help them understand why what they did was wrong. Help them understand why they owe you an apology for fixing their problem. Your child must also promise it won’t happen again.

When your child refuses to apologize

You must always ask what happened first before telling your child to say I’m sorry. Get both sides of the story. Because sometimes the wrong kid can cry 1st. then a parent will tell the one that is not crying to apologize.

This can make the child refuse to apologize. Force a child to say I’m sorry will never be a good idea.

Even when your child refuses to apologize. You must not yale or get angry. The aim is to teach our kids to respect others. We also need to respect our kids and understand learning to apologize is a long process.

The mistake parents do is to forget kids are growing. Yelling will make your kids fear you. Once your kids fear you it gets even harder to teach them. Sometimes it damages them mentally.

If your child is still angry and refusing to apologize. Give them time to cool down and speak with them.

Apologizing is difficult, and it is hard to even for most adults. Most of them were not thought to apologize the right way growing up.

Tips that can teach your kids to apologize

It is simple to apologize to your kids and those around you. When kids see you doing good, they will do the same.

Don’t force your kids to say I’m sorry. But help them understand how important it is to be sorry.

So that when they apologize it genuine and meaningful. This will also help them apologize, even when you are not around them.

What is a humble apology?

Our kids must understand apologizing doesn’t mean you are wrong. But you value your relationship with that person. Thus, they must be humble when they apologize. Because apologizing is not about them. It is about improving their friendship. The genuine apology will increase trust and authority.

Apologizing humble mean, you are truly sorry and you will do all that you could to make sure this won’t happen again.

Why must kids learn to apologize?

We need to teach our kids to apologize so they can protect their relationships with others.

The pleasant thing about kids learns to apologize at an early stage. They will use the same principles when they reach adult Hud.

Teaching kids to be sorry. Also, help them understand we all make mistakes. But we must take responsibility for our mistakes.

How to avoid Mother’s Day disappointment.

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date October 20, 2020
  • No Comments on How to avoid Mother’s Day disappointment.

Mothers deserve better. Where it is a stepmother or bio mom. They both deserve better. But somehow most mothers’ days don’t always go the way you might hope for. Even as a stepmother you deserve to be treated specially on Mother’s Day. Because even though you are a stepmother, you still love your partner’s kids. You also do sacrifices for them. They need to show you they appreciate all this. Even though you are not the real mother.

Lower your exceptions on a Mother’s Day

The first step in avoiding Mother’s Day disappointment is to lower your expectations. Especially if you are a stepmother. Even if you know you sacrifice a lot for kids. You must lower your expectation. A lot can go wrong. Mother’s Day can make your The Stepkids miss their bio mom. While you on the other side expect them to show you love during this time.

I understand things we see on TV, Radio, Social Media, Etc. can make us jealous and feel like our kids don’t love you or appreciate you. Always keep in mind that every situation is different. Kids are different and parents. Some kids are still developing and some are fully grown. Some kids love you. But don’t know how to express their love to you yet. We all process things differently. Even us as a parent sometimes doesn’t express love the way we should.

My mother passed away when I was young, and my grandmother raised me. Even though my father was still present. My grandmother was the one taking care of me. Everything I needed, my father would pass money on to my grandmother. But she was the one who gets things done. Sometimes she didn’t even tell my dad. She would buy whatever I need with her mother. Yet, there was never a day on Mother’s Day. Where I did something for her to show that I appreciate her sacrifices. Am I proud of that? No, I did not understand she was special until I was grown. You must forget what happening around you. Focus on your situation.

What role can father play on Mother’s Day?

If you are a father, you must also play your part. She is raising your kids. You appreciate that. If your kids are not planning anything. It must be your job to ask them. “what should we do for your mother this Mother’s Day?” help your kids see the need to do something special for their mother. You can even help your kids with the present. Also, fund them if needed. But if they are working, that even better. Normalize showing your wife or girlfriend how special they are always.

Celebrate yourself as a mother.

It nice to be celebrated. There is that amazing feeling if someone shows you, they love you. But you must teach yourself to celebrate you before anyone else. If it is Mother’s Day and you know the important role you play in your kids’ life. You know you are a splendid mother and you are proud.
Celebrate yourself and be proud these kids have gone this far because of you. Don’t expect too much on others to go out there and celebrate you as a mother.

Appreciate everything you get in a Mother’s Day

Don’t be a picky mom. Learn to appreciate everything you get where it big or small. Be thankful because it the thought that counts. Even those small gifts come from a good heart. You might get the bigger present the following new, who knows?

When issues are left unaddressed

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date October 15, 2020
  • No Comments on When issues are left unaddressed

You will have issues in your marriage and relationship. If you keep avoiding addressing your issues. This will create bigger problems in a long run.

If you experience issues in your relationship or marriage. normalize talking with your partner about them. Leaving them undressed will create doubt and anger about the relationship. The more issues you leave unaddressed. The bigger the bridge between you and your partner.

It doesn’t matter how old is that problem. If it is still bothering you talk about it. This can help you in the healing process. Because once you talk to your partner and they give you their side of the story. This is when you can decide whether you still want to hold it against them or let go.

Get your partner’s side of the story. Will sometimes make you realize how wrong you were about the situation. If this happens. You must forgive yourself and your partner. Learn more about the power of forgiveness for yourself and others.

Talking about your problems will not only help you in a healing process. This all builds a strong and lasting relationship. Create a strong connection in your relationship. You must never be in denial and avoid your issues. Problems will always be there; you need a good problem-solving skill. So, your relationship can always stay healthy.

Big warning though avoids making a big deal out of the small thing. Because I am saying you need to address things that bother you.

This doesn’t mean everything, something you can forgive your partner. Without even talking about them, but if it keeps eating you inside. Then you can address it. Learn more about making a big deal out of small things.

If something happened in your relationship. You need to know how did this impact you emotionally and physically. Did it also affect your partner or it you? If your partner is not addressing the issue. This doesn’t mean they are not hurting. Especially men like to be powerful people. That why most of the time they like to keep feelings to themselves. Then it keeps adding and adding until they can’t take anymore.

Men can wake up one day and tell you I don’t want this anymore. You won’t understand what happened. But it several things he did not address.

If you talk to your partner, they learn to open to you. Because your partner might hurt secretly. Even if he is hurting or you are hurting secretly. That anger will show itself one day. You might not even realize it connected to the previous unaddressed issues.

Old issues

Your relationship will not grow until you learn to talk. Avoid creating distance in your relationship. If your partner brings up an old issue that has been bothering him or her. You must never ask them why they want to talk about it now. Because until you address it. It will keep affecting the relationship.

You must take this as the opportunity to fix the previous issue. Stop wondering why now, fix it and move on. Because if your partner brought something up. This means there is more they want you to understand.

Yes, you won’t be able to fix all your issues. But talking about them will make your partner see you care, and you are listening. So, talking can heal your partner even when there was no solution. Being heard alone means more. Because your partner now knows if this happens again you will know how to act.

Stop leaving issue undressed. It will never be good for your relationship.

  • Ruffled feelings
  • sharp words
  • sudden outbursts

We should forget such storms in your family. They must not be allowed to leave any hint of unfriendly will. Sometimes we take family fusses too seriously, the issue over them, and allow them to darken the family’s happiness.
