What are the worst mistakes parents can make?

  • Post author By TheFamilies
  • Post date September 16, 2020
  • No Comments on What are the worst mistakes parents can make?

The mistake we do as parents is to think we can be perfect parent. When there is no such thing as perfect parenting. Because you are older, doesn’t mean you are always right. Some days you will say wrong things to your kids or be hash. Nothing wrong with going back to your kids and apologize.  You must never have the pride to apologise. Not only with your kids, but in life.

Learn more about how to apologise and when.

Every parent makes mistakes, and every kid makes mistakes. You will make mistakes there is no way to prevent them. You can only reduce and cut your mistakes. But you can’t prevent them.

Beliefs we have can make us parent the wrong way. We must be open to learning new things and changed as the world change. Trying to raise your kids the same way you grew up won’t work. If your parents punished you a lot as a kid. Which made a powerful man. Don’t think it is wise to raise your kids that way. Beliefs are what will make us do most mistakes.

If you have a certain style of parenting and you see it not working. Do not force it, learn something else. Notice everything that is not working. Drop your ego, understand there is so much you don’t know.

let look at what are the worst mistakes parents can make.

Not choosing your words wisely.

Words are powerful, and they can build or destroy. You must be careful about what you say to your children. My mom passed away when I was very young, and there are things my stepmother said to me. When I was 9 years old, about my bio mother. Which I carry to this day. Because they were mean things to say to a child.

Words as parents sometimes can come out the wrong way. Words have a deep meaning. They can heal and be weapons.

Talking too much with your kids.

Avoid talking too much with your kids. Say things you want to say and give them time to process everything. If you spend 30 minutes talking to your kids. They won’t remember everything, only the thing which makes sense to them. You don’t have to say the same things over and over for kids to understand you. If you do that it feels like abuse they heard the 1st time. Yet you still repeating it over and over.

.Less is more! The worst mistakes parents make when talking to kids. Not paying attention to a kid’s needs.

Not realizing how expensive babies can be.

You are going to be the one carrying for your baby. Know your financial status and understand if you are ready. If you have over one kid. They can get too expensive and if you have no plan. You will experience problems. You might even be angry at kids for destroying your budget. While it is you who had no birth plan.

With babies, we don’t know exactly when are they coming. But we need to save as early as possible. So, when that day comes, we are ready. It is difficult to save after you have a baby. It much easier if you save before you have a child.

Neglecting kids’ hunger

You can’t expect your kids to be at their best while their tummy is empty. Hunger makes it hard to concentrate and can escalate misbehaving. Even us as parents we are likely to be impatient when we are hungry.  Parents sometimes force kids to go out and play with other kids. Therefore, they can avoid making food for them. If you do that stop it now, it is not good for your child.

It your job to make sure your kids eat at all times. If their appetite drop take that seriously.

Giving your kids Alcohol

Parents have a tendency of making kids taste alcohol. When a child start acts odd. they think it hilarious and take videos to post on social media. The following tweet is the great example of bad parenting.

I have watched this 35 times pic.twitter.com/QNusyBCYG0

— Rupert Myers (@RupertMyers) October 13, 2020

You must choose your kids over alcohol. If you allow your kids to taste alcohol. You are the worst parent and they must arrest you. The drinking age is 18 years old here in South Africa, and in some parts of the world, it is 21 years old. There is a reason for this.

I see this a lot, even in my family. I never understand. How can you do such a thing to your child? What lessons are you giving them? I just don’t get it!

There is a lot you can do with your kids. Things like helping them learn to share. Instead of giving them alcohol.

Shouting at your kids

You must avoid freaking out for your kids. Shouting undermines your ability to get your words through. They either shut down or get mad in response to your shouting. Shouting at your kids also makes them feel you hate them. They also feel like the worst kids and hate themselves. Avoid shouting!

Try to talk some sense with them without rising your voice.

Blocking your kids from technology

There is a debate about smartphones and laptops. Parents have a fear of buying smartphones for their children. They might play games all the time and stop doing school work. Or watch unsuitable content for their age and more. This is all true!

But let stop focusing on what can go wrong. Think about what technology can bring to your child. How I usually do this, when I’m thinking about buying a smartphone for my child. Yet, I’m worried they might use it the wrong way.

First, I buy a new phone and give a child the old one. When I give my child the old one, I never tell them it theirs. “This is my old phone, you can use it. Because I have a new one. But if I see this phone is making you lose focus on important things like school. I will take it away.”

While I know the phone is no longer mine. Yet I don’t let the kid know that. So, they can give it some respect.

Same thing with laptops. I never give them 100% ownership. But the worst thing you can do is to block your children from using technology. I try to teach my kids everything that can help them be independent. Like how to invest in the stock market. Blogging and everything that they can start as a hobby. But in a log run generate good income.  Would it be nice to see your kids making their 1st $1000 at 16? Wouldn’t that be great?

Shaming your kids

Shaming kids is another worst mistake parent do. If your child did something wrong. There is no need to compare your child with the one that is doing great. By doing this, and it will make your kids hate one another. Because you make them think. The other one is bad, and the other is the best. Parents also expect kids to do things the way their parents taught them. If they fail, then they see them as losers. Stop shaming your kids, it is wrong. Give them time to make mistakes and learn their own mistake.

The following video help us with ways we unknowingly shame our children.

Parents fighting with kids

We see this almost everywhere. When after parents broke up, they fighting using kids. Kids have nothing to do with our breakups. Kids deserve both parents, even when those parents are no longer together. A child must have a relationship with both parents.

I experienced this a lot with my 1st child’s mother. She would try to get back to me and if that didn’t work out. She would cut me out completely, to a point where it would be impossible to contact both. That was painful to me, and I’m sure it was for my daughter.

Parents need to stop involving kids in their personal problems. Love your children more than you dislike each other.

Don’t make the mistake to not be the part of your child growing up.

Be a leading example to your kids

I think I have talked about this before in one of my posts. How kids can be copycats. You need to be a leading example in everything you want them to be. If you want them to treat everyone with respect. You must be the one treating your family with respect first. Kids learn more when they watch you doing something. Then when they listen to you talking about it. 

If you want more kindness in the world, put some there.

Not teaching your kids boundaries

Families must have boundaries and kids too. You must train children to do a certain thing and to act in certain ways. Example, your kids must knock every time they enter your bedroom. If you don’t teach them this as early as possible. One day they will see things you don’t want them to see and they will humble you.

Setting restrictions teach our kids to respect and know their limits.

Never be your child’s best friend

Kids need to understand you are not a friend. But a parent, if they have problems. They must come to you. If you have problems, you won’t come to them, but their father. It is very rare that you will tell them your secrets. However, it is not okay for them to keep a secret from you. They are not allowed to use your things without your person.

Long story short, we can be close. But not too close to a point where there are no boundaries.

Expecting your child to fulfill your dreams

We all have things we wanted to do growing up. Which we didn’t, no need to force our kids to do things we failed to do ourselves. You can try to teach them if you feel is important and can help them. However, if you see, they are not into it. Don’t force them, let them pick their own system of life.
